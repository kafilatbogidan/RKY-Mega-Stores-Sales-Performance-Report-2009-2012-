# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard Analysis (Excel Project)

## 🏢 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive *Sales Performance Dashboard* built using Microsoft Excel for *RKY Mega Stores, covering the period **2009 – 2012*.  

The goal of this analysis is to transform raw sales data into *actionable insights* that support decision-making across operations, customer management, and logistics.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing product categories and regions
- Evaluate customer segments profitability
- Monitor return rates and shipping costs
- Highlight key business opportunities and inefficiencies

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- *Microsoft Excel*
  - Pivot Tables
  - Charts & Visualizations
  - Slicers (for interactivity)
  - Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 🔹 Sales by Product Category
- *Technology* generated the highest revenue (£5.98M)
- *Furniture* followed closely (£5.18M)
- *Office Supplies* recorded the lowest (£3.75M)

👉 Insight: Technology products are the primary revenue driver and should be prioritized in marketing and inventory.

---

### 🌍 Sales by Region
- *West region* leads with £3.60M in sales
- *Ontario and Prairie* also show strong performance
- *Nunavut* recorded the lowest sales (£0.12M)

👉 Insight: There is a significant regional imbalance. Underperforming regions may require targeted strategies.

---

### 🚚 Shipping Cost Analysis
- *Delivery Truck* is the most used and costly (£52K)
- *Regular Air* follows (£48K)
- *Express Air* is least used (£8K)

👉 Insight: Heavy reliance on delivery trucks suggests potential for logistics cost optimization.

---

### 👥 Profit by Customer Segment
- *Corporate segment* generated the highest profit (£508K)
- Followed by:
  - Home Office (£296K)
  - Small Business (£257K)
  - Consumer (£263K)

👉 Insight: Corporate customers are the most valuable and should be retained through loyalty strategies.

---

### 🔁 Returned Orders by Category
- *Office Supplies* has the highest returns (461)
- Technology (218) and Furniture (193) follow

👉 Insight: High return rates in Office Supplies may indicate quality or fulfillment issues.

---

### 🏆 Top 10 Valuable Customers
The dashboard highlights key customers such as:

---

## 📈 Key Takeaways
- Technology is the strongest revenue driver
- Corporate customers are the most profitable segment
- Certain regions underperform and need attention
- Office Supplies has a high return rate (risk area)
- Logistics costs can be optimized

---

## 🚀 Recommendations
- Increase investment in high-performing categories (Technology)
- Develop targeted strategies for low-performing regions
- Investigate causes of high product returns
- Optimize shipping methods to reduce costs
- Strengthen relationships with top customers
